steam team

Steam team is a design group from South Lakes High School, Reston Virginia, who creates temporary sculptures for display on the nearby Lake Thoreau. Started in 2013, the team has completed multiple successful works of public art. The student members in steam team bring a sculpture idea from conception till construction.

Simon, the sculpture for 2016, was designed by the team off a concept by Joshua. The following images are concept art made by him and also the final sculpture at the end.
